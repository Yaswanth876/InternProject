# Altitude vs. temperature

    print(f"At {alt} m: {temp}°C")

ğŸ§© Think and Decide

  • Why does the temperature drop as you climb a mountain?
    Answer: Air gets thinner and cooler at higher altitudes.
  • If the lapse rate is 1°C per 200 m, how much cooler is Ooty vs. Chennai?
    Answer: (2200 m–50 m)/200 m ≈ 10.75°C, close to observed 15°C drop.
  • Which would be more pleasant for a hot summer day: a hill or a plain? Why?
    Answer: Hill station, due to the lower temperature at a higher altitude.
